Anglo Concertina Fingering Generator

Batch Pre-processor for ABC Tunebooks

Instructions:

Click "Open an existing .ABC file" to open a file or paste one or more ABC tunes into the box below.

Enter your desired fingering tablature font sizes, %%staffsep, and %%musicspace values.

Click "Generate Fingerings".

The generated fingerings will appear in the "Processed ABC Output, with Fingerings" box.

Click "Save Output to a file" to save the results to a file.

Click "Copy Output to the clipboard" to copy the results to the system clipboard for pasting into other applications.

Click "Test Output in the ABC Transcription Tools" to instantly open and test the results in my ABC Transcription Tools

ABC Input:

  Annotate bellows directions ( ↓ = Push, ↑ = Draw )

  Inject %%MIDI directives to mute bass/chords

Font family:Recommended: Palatino

Title font size:Recommended: 22

Subtitle font size:Recommended: 18

Info font size:Recommended: 14

Tab font size:Recommended: Reels/Hornpipes - 9, Jigs - 11

%%staffsep value:Recommended: 80

%%musicspace value:Recommended: 10

Notes

Only supports C/G Jeffies and Wheatstone layouts.

Chooses combinations to avoid "hopping" and to try to stay on the home rows.

Known Issues

Fails when converting multi-voice tunes:

For best results, use source ABC tunes that have a single voice. (i.e. no V: tags)

Unpredictable results or a failure to complete the fingering generation may occur with multi-voice ABC files.

Button direction shows up as "null" for some less-often used accidentals:

After fingering generation, some less-often used accidentals like ^A may display "null" as their direction in the ABC.

If you see "null" as the direction for any note, review the ABC in the ABC editor and correct the direction manually by copying and pasting either a push "↓" or draw "↑" arrow in place of the "null" before generating a tunebook.
