Anglo Concertina Fingering Generator

Batch Pre-processor for ABC Tunebooks

Notes:

Only supports C/G Jeffies and Wheatstone layouts.

Chooses combinations to avoid "hopping" and to try to stay on the home rows.

Fingering choices are still not optimal. For example, bellows usage is ignored. More work is needed here.

For best results, use source ABC files that do not already contain %%MIDI directives or chords.

Unpredictable results may occur if %MIDI directives or chords are already present.

Instructions:

Paste one or more ABC tunes into the box below.

Enter your desired fingering tablature font sizes, %%staffsep, and %%musicspace values.

Click "Generate Fingerings".

Select and copy the resulting annotated ABC to your clipboard and paste it into my ABC Transcription Tools to render the final tunebook.

ABC Input:

Open a .ABC file:  

  Annotate bellows directions ('P'=Push, 'D'=Draw)

  Inject %%MIDI directives to mute bass/chords

Font family:Recommended: Palatino

Title font size:Recommended: 22

Subtitle font size:Recommended: 18

Info font size:Recommended: 14

Tab font size:Recommended: Reels - 8, Jigs - 10

%%staffsep value:Recommended: 80

%%musicspace value:Recommended: 10
