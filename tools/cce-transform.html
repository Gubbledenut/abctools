Comhaltas ABC Notation Transformer

Batch Pre-processor for ABC Tunebooks

Converts d' e' f' style ABC to D' E' F' style

Instructions:  Jump to Tool

Important: While the note Names display in the ABC Transcription Tool will show the Comhaltas-preferred note names, the ABC standard requires that the second octave C in the ABC itself either be represented by c or C' so that may requires some minor changes when pasting in your Comhaltas-formatted tunes.

1) Click "Open an existing .ABC file" to open a file or paste one or more ABC tunes into the box below.

2) Click "Transform ABC".

The transformed ABC will appear in the "Processed ABC Output, with Tablature" box.

3) Save, Copy or Test the results:

Click "Save Output to a file" to save the results to a file.

Click "Copy Output to the clipboard" to copy the results to the system clipboard for pasting into other applications.

Click "Test Output in the ABC Transcription Tools" to open and test the results in my ABC Transcription Tools
